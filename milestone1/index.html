What content will the site contain?

My site will contian yogic and minful practices that I feel called to share throughout my yoga teacher training.

Where will you get all of the content for your site?

Most of my content will come from things I am learning in my modules and classes as well as my own thoughts and meditation topics I come up with.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

I think users should care so they can decide if they feel called to join me in my journey.

Why should a site be built for this?

I think making more approachable entrences into yoga and mindfullness helps brings others to practices that have helped me so much.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to join my jouney as I learn more about yoga and hopefully they can interact and learn as well as teach me a thing or two.

What is the goal of the site?

I would hope that my sight would attract people that are either starting their own yoga journey and need information, attract students when I start teaching, and create community.
