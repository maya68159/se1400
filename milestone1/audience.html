Audience

Geolocation

I would hope my webiste would appeal to people from all over. I imagine that it would grasp more that are from the United States and English speaking as thats probably the location that would most understand my content.

Age

My site would probably attract users of ages similar to mine as thats the audience I, by default, communicate most effectively with.

Gender

I imagine I will attract more women than men users, but I intend to make a website that is welcoming and accessable to those of any gender or orientation.

Overall, I imagine my site will attract female users in locations similar to mine, that speak the same language as me, and around my same age. However, it is very important to me to put energy into creating a site that is welcoming to all, especially those that don't normally feel welcome in the yoga community and those with disabilities.
